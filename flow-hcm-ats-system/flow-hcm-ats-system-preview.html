We are excited to announce that we are upgrading our recruitment infrastructure by moving from Oracle ATS to Flow HCM. This transition is a key step in streamlining our hiring processes and improving the experience for both candidates and hiring managers.

Key Transition Timelines

⛔️ Oracle ATS Decommissioning
All services on Oracle ATS will be officially shut down as of April 1st, 2026.
 
✅ Flow HCM Go-Live
We are working diligently on the migration and will provide a formal launch date shortly.
Interim Manual Process:

To ensure our hiring momentum does not stall during this transition, we will move to a manual approval process starting April 1st. Please note the following:
Requisitions & Approvals: All new job requisitions and candidate stage approvals shall be submitted via email by the Recruitment Team.
We recognize that transitions of this nature require adaptability, and we appreciate your partnership in maintaining momentum while we implement a more agile and scalable solution.
We will continue to provide updates as we approach go-live and ensure all stakeholders are equipped for a smooth transition.
